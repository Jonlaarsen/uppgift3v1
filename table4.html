Story

The Archangel Tyrael is a member of the legendary Angiris Council. He is the Archangel of Justice, and an increasingly prominent and popular icon of the entire Diablo franchise. According to Deckard Cain, Tyrael "was renowned for being calm and controlled and meticulous in his execution of combat technique." Tyrael's weapon is the legendary El'druin, the Sword of Justice, and he was known in the ages before the rise of man as being at all times fair and impartial, as Justice should be.

Story

The Butcher was an infamous demon who twice plagued Khanduras. A bloated and grotesque creature, The Butcher was a sadistic being that relished in the torture and pain of others. It was also masochistic, and its own hands were damaged as the result of its torture. It hunted endlessly for fresh meat, apparently preferring it raw. Both Kharazim and Valla noted the creature's strength, the latter commenting that the Butcher "was one of the most powerful creatures I have ever faced."
